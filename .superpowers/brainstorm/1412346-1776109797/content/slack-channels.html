Recommended Slack Channel Structures

Three options — pick one or mix elements. Click your preference.

A

Minimal (3 channels)

Best for: simplicity, small team, low Slack noise

Channel Who Posts Purpose
#volunteer-log Bot (all members) Task completions via QR + OCR; everyone can see
#shop-notices Bot only Reminders, new sheets, monthly summaries
#shop-manage 🔒 Stewards+ All admin: task CRUD, reports, OCR photos, queries

Pros

  • Fewest channels to manage
  • Members only watch 2 channels

Cons

  • #shop-manage mixes commands + OCR photos
  • No private alert channel for errors
B

Balanced (5 channels) — Recommended

Best for: clear separation, crew of ~50 members

Channel Access Purpose
#volunteer-log All members (read)
Bot (write)		 Task completions posted by bot when QR scanned; builds social proof
#shop-bulletin All members (read)
Bot (write)		 Announcements: new sign-up sheets, reminders, monthly summaries
#shop-queries All members Natural language queries to the bot ("how many tasks did I complete?", "what's still open in metalshop?")
#shop-admin 🔒 Stewards+ Task CRUD commands, report generation, OCR photo intake, sheet generation triggers
#shop-alerts 🔒 Shop Sergeant Bot errors, compliance flags, anomalies, audit trail

Pros

  • Members interact in one place (#shop-queries)
  • Admins have clean workspace (#shop-admin)
  • Silent error channel protects UX

Cons

  • 5 channels to set up and explain
C

Granular (6 channels)

Best for: high message volume, audit-heavy needs

Channel Access Purpose
#volunteer-log All / Bot QR-triggered completions only
#shop-bulletin All / Bot Announcements and reminders
#shop-queries All members Member queries to bot
#task-intake 🔒 Stewards+ OCR photo uploads only — keeps admin photo stream separate
#shop-admin 🔒 Stewards+ Task CRUD, reports, sheet generation
#shop-alerts 🔒 Shop Sergeant Errors, flags, audit trail

Pros

  • OCR photos isolated from text commands
  • Cleanest audit trail

Cons

  • 6 channels; admins manage two restricted spaces

🔒 = restricted to stewards, shop steward, and shop sergeant only
