Proposed KPIs for Shop Sergeant

Select all that belong in the requirements doc. Add others in the terminal.

M1

Member Compliance Rate

% of active members who logged ≥ 2 volunteer hours in a given month.
Target: ≥ 80% of members compliant each month

M2

Task Completion Rate

% of posted work tasks that are completed within their scheduled period.
Target: ≥ 90% of tasks completed on schedule

M3

Data Completeness Rate

% of completed tasks that have a member name, date, and task ID recorded in Google Sheets.
Target: ≥ 95% complete records (vs. current near-0%)

M4

Capture Friction Score

Average number of taps/steps for a member to log a completed task via mobile.
Target: ≤ 2 taps from QR scan to confirmation

O1

Steward Adoption Rate

% of stewards actively using the system to manage tasks (vs. paper only).
Target: 100% of stewards using system within 60 days of launch

O2

Sheet Refresh Latency

Time between a steward requesting a new sign-up sheet and it being physically posted.
Target: ≤ 24 hours

O3

Overdue Task Alert Rate

% of overdue tasks that triggered an automated reminder before going unnoticed.
Target: 100% of overdue tasks generate a Slack alert

O4

Top Contributor Recognition

Monthly report of top-contributing members generated and shared in #shop-bulletin.
Target: 100% on-time delivery each month

M = Member-facing metrics  |  O = Operational metrics
Click to select, then tell me in the terminal if you want to adjust targets or add more.
