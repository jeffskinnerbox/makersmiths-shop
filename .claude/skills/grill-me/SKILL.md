Think Hard about what must be done,
and interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding.
Walk down each branch of the plan / design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one.

While presenting to me one question at a time, providing me several possible answers, your recommendation,
and an opportunity for me to provide my own answer (if it applies).
Put your possible answers in a list and give me the capability to tab to my chosen answer and select it.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase,
explore the codebase first, and then ask the question.
