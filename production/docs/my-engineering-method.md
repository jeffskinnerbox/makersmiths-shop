An **engineering method** is a structured, iterative,
and heuristic-driven process used to solve complex,
often ill-defined problems by applying scientific principles,
engineering knowledge, and mathematical analysis to create efficient solutions.
It involves defining problems, brainstorming, prototyping, testing,
and refining solutions, often iterating through these steps to optimize results

* [Engineering Method](https://rpubs.com/Educatus/Engineering_Methods)
* [The Scientific Method vs. Engineering Design Process](https://www.msubillings.edu/scienceexpo/methods.htm)
* [Engineering Method](https://sites.tufts.edu/eeseniordesignhandbook/2013/engineering-method/)
